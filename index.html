About Us

Spice Bite is a pure vegetarian restaurant located in the heart of Dubai. Our menu combines traditional Indian favourites with modern twists and Chinese delights. We pride ourselves on using fresh, high‑quality ingredients prepared in a hygienic environment. Whether you’re craving quick bites, hearty mains or sweet endings, Spice Bite offers something special for every vegetarian food lover.
